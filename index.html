About Us

What we do

Sydney League is an alumni-run association founded in 2014, that provides a competitive esports platform for the Sydney Technical High School student and alumni community. Relaunched in August 2017, we run occasional tournaments and events.

The league is coordinated by the organisers of the 2016 Sydney Technical High School SRC Counter-Strike Championships by TteSports.

Major 2017

The event

This Counter-Strike: Global Offensive tournament is a two stage event. Eight teams will compete in a round robin stage to decide seeding, and then all particpants proceed to the playoffs, where the teams will play in a single eliminations finals stage. This is the second major event coordinated by your organisers — and the first event in a year!

The event guide can be accessed here. The official rulebook can be found here.

Major Championship Series

Major Championship Series are the largest events organised by Sydney League, and are run annually/biannually. Look out for the Counter-Strike: Global Offensive Pro League series, starting early next year.

Entry Details

The tournament is for Sydney Tech student and alumni Counter-Strike: Global Offensive teams. Students can register as Sydney League members here. Teams of five may register for the event here. The entry fee is $20pp. The event is not-for-profit and all contributions will be used for prizes, server hire, and broadcast equipment, for future tournaments and events.

Map Pool

Major Championship Series Summer 2017 will include five official maps as part of its map pool of seven in total: Inferno (new), Dust II (new), Cobblestone, Train (new), and Cache — the two community maps include Tuscan (Toscan), and Torn (Torntown).
